Belgium Company Car Tax & Tesla in 2026

Belgium has one of Europe’s most attractive EV company car regimes. Since the 2021 reform, the benefit-in-kind (voordeel van alle aard / avantage de toute nature) for fully electric company cars is far lower than for combustion vehicles — making a Tesla Model Y or Model 3 as a company car extremely cost-effective for employers and employees alike.

  • Benefit-in-kind (BIK): EVs sit at 0g CO²/km, giving the minimum BIK rate. The taxable benefit for an EV is a fraction of the equivalent combustion vehicle.
  • Corporate deductibility: EVs ordered before 2027 retain 100% deductibility for Belgian corporate tax — a significant incentive for businesses.
  • Mobility Budget: Belgium’s Mobility Budget scheme (Budget Mobilité) can include EVs. A Tesla may be available through your employer’s mobility scheme.
  • Regional incentives: Walloon and Flemish subsidies may apply depending on your region. Check with a Belgian tax advisor for current rules.

Whatever route you use — personal purchase, company car, leasing, or salary sacrifice — referral code carlo719460 applies at the order stage.
