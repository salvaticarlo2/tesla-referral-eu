Tesla in Switzerland: What Buyers Should Know in 2026

Swiss Tesla Pricing (CHF)

Tesla Switzerland prices include 8.1% Swiss VAT (MWST). Because Switzerland is outside the EU, prices are set independently and can differ from neighbouring markets. In general, Swiss prices track closely with German prices converted at prevailing CHF/EUR rates, though the lower Swiss VAT (8.1% vs Germany's 19%) means the sticker price gap is smaller than it appears.

Swiss Supercharger Network

Switzerland has one of Europe's most complete Supercharger networks relative to its size. Key locations include:

  • Zürich (Altstetten, Wallisellen, multiple city locations)
  • Geneva and Lausanne (Romandie coverage)
  • Basel (cross-border corridor)
  • Gotthard corridor (A2 motorway — Airolo, Biasca, Lugano for Ticino trips)
  • Graubünden (Chur, Davos, St. Moritz — critical for winter mountain access)

Your 1,500 km of free referral Supercharging covers roughly 15–20 typical Swiss charging sessions.

Cantonal EV Incentives

Switzerland has no national EV purchase subsidy, but several cantons offer incentives:

  • Geneva (GE): Cantonal bonus for zero-emission vehicles
  • Vaud (VD): Vehicle tax exemptions for EVs
  • Zurich (ZH): Reduced cantonal Motorfahrzeugsteuer for EVs

Check your canton before ordering. Combined with the CHF 500 referral benefit, total savings can be substantial.
