Who Should Buy Enhanced Autopilot

Enhanced Autopilot is the right choice if:

  • You mostly drive on motorways. Your commute is highway-heavy and Navigate on Autopilot covers 80% of your driving.
  • You want to save money now. At €3,800 vs €7,500, EAP is half the price. If budget matters, it delivers excellent value.
  • FSD is not available in your country yet. If you are in Germany, France, or Italy waiting for mutual recognition, EAP gives you automation today. You can upgrade to FSD later when it launches.
  • You prefer to wait and see. FSD Supervised is new in Europe. If you want to let others test it first, start with EAP and upgrade when you are ready.

Who Should Buy FSD Supervised

FSD Supervised is worth the premium if:

  • You drive in cities regularly. Navigating traffic lights, roundabouts, and intersections is where FSD earns its value. If your daily drive includes urban stretches, FSD makes a real difference.
  • You are in the Netherlands. FSD is live now. You can use city street driving today — no waiting.
  • You want the complete experience. FSD handles the entire journey. No disengaging Autopilot when you exit the motorway and enter city traffic.
  • You plan to keep the car long-term. The one-time purchase price becomes more economical over time compared to monthly subscriptions. Over 3+ years, the €3,700 upgrade pays for itself vs subscribing monthly.

The Smart Strategy: Buy EAP Now, Subscribe FSD to Test

Not sure? Here is the most cost-effective approach for European buyers in 2026:

  1. Buy Enhanced Autopilot with your new Tesla (€3,800). You get highway automation immediately.
  2. Subscribe to FSD for one month when it launches in your country (~€99–199). Test city street driving on your actual routes.
  3. Decide based on experience. If FSD changes your daily drive, upgrade permanently. If not, keep EAP and save €3,700.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Tesla FSD and Enhanced Autopilot in Europe?

Enhanced Autopilot includes highway features: Navigate on Autopilot, auto lane change, autopark, smart summon, and traffic-aware cruise control. FSD Supervised adds city street driving — it navigates intersections, roundabouts, traffic lights, and stop signs. EAP handles motorways; FSD handles the entire journey.

Can I upgrade from Enhanced Autopilot to FSD later?

Yes. Tesla allows upgrades from Enhanced Autopilot to FSD at any time through the Tesla app or your online account. The upgrade price is the difference between FSD and EAP pricing (~€3,700). You can also subscribe to FSD monthly to test it before buying.

Is Tesla FSD worth it in Europe in 2026?

It depends on your driving. If you mostly use motorways, Enhanced Autopilot at ~€3,800 covers your needs. If you drive in cities regularly — navigating traffic lights, roundabouts, and intersections — FSD Supervised adds real value. Try the monthly subscription first to see if it fits your routine.

How much does FSD cost compared to Enhanced Autopilot in Europe?

Enhanced Autopilot costs approximately €3,800 one-time. FSD Supervised is expected around €7,500 one-time or €99–199/month as a subscription. The upgrade from EAP to FSD costs about €3,700.

Does Enhanced Autopilot work on city streets in Europe?

No. Enhanced Autopilot is limited to highways and motorways. For city street navigation — traffic lights, roundabouts, intersections — you need FSD Supervised, which launched in the Netherlands in March 2026 and is rolling out across the EU.
