Portugal EV Incentives 2026

Portugal has one of the more EV-friendly fiscal regimes in Southern Europe. Here's what's available alongside the Tesla referral code:

🏦 IRSF EV Subsidy

Portugal's Incentivo à Aquisição de Veículos de Baixas Emissões provides purchase subsidies for new BEVs. Check the IAPMEI portal for current amounts — these update annually.

🚗 IUC Tax Exemption

Electric vehicles are fully exempt from Portugal's annual road tax (Imposto Único de Circulação / IUC), saving hundreds of euros per year.

💼 Company Car Benefit

EVs in Portugal benefit from lower benefit-in-kind taxation vs combustion cars — making a Tesla an attractive company vehicle choice.
