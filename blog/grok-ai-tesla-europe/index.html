What Is Grok AI?

Grok is a large language model (LLM) â€” the same fundamental technology behind ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini â€” developed specifically by xAI. It was trained on real-time data from X (formerly Twitter) in addition to broad internet content, giving it a strong grasp of current events and a notably direct, sometimes irreverent personality.

You can ask it anything, in your own words, and it will understand context, follow-up questions, and ambiguous requests.

The name "Grok" is borrowed from Robert Heinlein's 1961 science fiction novel Stranger in a Strange Land, where it means to understand something so deeply that you absorb it and become part of it.
