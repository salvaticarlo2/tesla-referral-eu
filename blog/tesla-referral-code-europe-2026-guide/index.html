What Is the Tesla Referral Program?

Tesla's referral program lets existing owners share a unique referral link with people about to order a new Tesla. When someone uses that link to complete a purchase, both the referrer and the new buyer receive rewards. The program has evolved many times but remains one of the most generous in the automotive industry.

What is the Tesla Loot Box?

Tesla renamed its referral rewards interface to the Loot Box in a 2023 app update. It's simply where your referral credits live after delivery. In the Tesla app, go to your account → Loot Box to see your current Supercharging credit balance, expiry dates, and any Tesla account credits you've received. The credits apply automatically when you charge at a Supercharger — no action required on your part. Free km is deducted before your paid balance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I add a referral code after I've already ordered?

Generally no. Contact Tesla support immediately — there is a small window where they may apply it retroactively.

Can I use more than one referral code?

No. Only one referral code per order.

Do referral credits expire?

Typically 12 months from delivery. Check your Tesla app under "Loot Box" or "Referral Rewards".

Does the referral benefit lock in at order or delivery?

At order time. Whatever was offered when you placed the order is what you receive — even if Tesla changes the program before delivery.

Can I use a referral code on a used or inventory Tesla?

No. Referral codes apply only to new vehicle orders through Tesla's official website.
