Privacy Policy

Updated March 15, 2026

TeslaBlog.eu is an independent informational website about Tesla in Europe. This page explains what data the site may process when you visit it and what happens when you follow referral links to Tesla.

What This Site Collects

This site does not offer user accounts, comments, uploads, or checkout. In normal use, the only data collected directly by the site is standard website traffic data such as page views, browser type, device information, and approximate location at the country or region level.

Analytics

TeslaBlog.eu uses Google Analytics 4 to understand which pages are useful and how visitors navigate the site. Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies to measure visits, traffic sources, and page performance.

Important: Analytics helps improve content quality and site usability, but it is not used to create user accounts or sell your personal data.

Referral Links

Some pages on this site contain Tesla referral links, including the referral code carlo719460. If you click one of those links, you leave TeslaBlog.eu and continue on Tesla's own website. At that point, Tesla's privacy policy and Tesla's data processing practices apply.

Cookies

This site may use basic analytics-related cookies through Google Analytics. It does not run advertising networks, user login cookies, or remarketing systems of its own.

Embedded or Third-Party Content

When pages link out to Tesla or other external sources, those sites operate under their own privacy policies. TeslaBlog.eu is not responsible for third-party privacy practices.

Data Sharing

TeslaBlog.eu does not sell visitor data. Information collected through analytics is used only to monitor site performance and improve articles and guides.

Your Choices

  • You can block analytics cookies in your browser settings.
  • You can avoid using referral links if you prefer to navigate directly to Tesla's website.
  • You can use browser privacy tools or extensions to limit third-party tracking.

Changes to This Policy

This page may be updated when site tooling, analytics, or referral handling changes. The date at the top of the page reflects the latest revision.
