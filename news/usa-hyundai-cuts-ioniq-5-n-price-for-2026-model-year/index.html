USA: Hyundai cuts Ioniq 5 N price for 2026 model year

Jul 22, 2026 · Source: electrive

Hyundai has reduced the starting price of the 2026 Ioniq 5 N in the US by $6,300 while introducing several technical updates. The performance EV now comes standard with a NACS charging port, bundled charging adapters and a revised drift mode.
