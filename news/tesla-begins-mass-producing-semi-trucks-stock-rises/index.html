Apr 30, 2026 · Source: Yahoo Finance TSLA

Tesla's dedicated high-volume production line at its Sparks, Nev., facility successfully manufactured its first Semi trucks. Wednesday's announcement, made via an X post, caps a nearly decade-long wait for mass production of Tesla's commercial trucks, which were first shown the public in 2017.
