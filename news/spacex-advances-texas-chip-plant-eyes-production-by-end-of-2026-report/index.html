SpaceX Advances Texas Chip Plant, Eyes Production By End Of 2026: Report

Apr 12, 2026 · Source: Yahoo Finance TSLA

SpaceX has reportedly initiated equipment installation at its advanced chip packaging facility in Bastrop, Texas. The company aims to begin production by the end of 2026, Reuters reported on Friday, citing sources. The aerospace giant is moving to bring semiconductor processes in-house.
