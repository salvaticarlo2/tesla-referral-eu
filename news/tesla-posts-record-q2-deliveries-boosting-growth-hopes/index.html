Jul 03, 2026 · Source: Yahoo Finance TSLA

STORY: Tesla posted record second-quarter deliveries on Thursday. The automaker beat Wall Street estimates and boosted hopes that it could end its two-year streak of annual sales declines. The company delivered more than 480,000 vehicles between April and June, up about 25% from a year ago.
