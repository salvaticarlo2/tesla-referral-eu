Leapmotor opens orders for B03X starting at €24,900

Jul 07, 2026 · Source: electrive

Leapmotor has opened order books in Europe for the B03X electric compact car. Presented to European markets in January, the electric model starts at €24,900 or from €239 per month on lease. This includes an 'Urban Crossover' with up to 382km range.
