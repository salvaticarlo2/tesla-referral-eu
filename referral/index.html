How to Use the Tesla Referral Code

Three steps, takes under a minute. The referral must be active and applied before you configure your vehicle.

01

Click the referral link

Go to tesla.com/referral/[code] using the referral link from a Tesla owner. This opens the Tesla Design Studio with the referral code already applied. Do not start from the regular tesla.com homepage — the referral won't attach.

02

Configure your Tesla

Choose your model (Model 3, Y, S or X), colour, wheels, and interior. You'll see the referral benefit shown in the order summary — either a direct discount or free Supercharging depending on the model.

03

Place your order

Complete checkout as normal. If the referral is active, benefits are confirmed at order and credited to your Tesla account at delivery.

Frequently Asked Questions

What do I get with a Tesla referral code in Europe?

When a referral is active: Tesla may offer buyer benefits such as credits, charging benefits, or other promotions. Benefits vary by country, model, and date; verify the active benefit in the Tesla app or configurator. Important: each owner has a yearly referral limit. This site's code is capped for 2026, so verify an active referral in the Tesla app before ordering.

Can I apply a referral code after placing my order?

No. The referral must be active before you place your order. Once submitted, Tesla does not allow referral codes to be added retroactively. Always start from the referral link before configuring your vehicle.

Does the referral work if I order from a different country than where I live?

Generally no. Tesla's configurator is tied to the country/region from which you order. Use the referral link and select your correct country within the Tesla website — the code will apply as long as it's active in your country.

How long do the Supercharging credits last?

Free Supercharging kilometres and Tesla credits earned through referral typically expire 12 months after being awarded (usually at delivery). Use them within that window.
