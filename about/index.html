About.

One European Tesla owner, a small amount of honest automation, and a set of rules this site is not allowed to break.

INDEPENDENT · NOT AFFILIATED WITH TESLA, INC.
01 Who

TeslaBlog.eu is written and run by Carlo, a Tesla owner based in Europe. I have owned and daily-driven Tesla vehicles across two generations, which is where the buying guides, the accessory reviews, and the practical bias of this site come from. This is an independent one-person project: no publisher, no content agency, no affiliation with Tesla, Inc.

02 How this site is made

Honestly, since most sites hide this: the news feed is aggregated automatically from 11 named sources four times a day, and those raw news pages are deliberately excluded from search engines. Articles are researched and drafted with AI assistance under a strict quality gate: every important claim needs a source link, uncertain claims do not get published, and a human owns every word that goes live. If an article cannot meet that bar, it is not published that week. One good article beats ten generated ones.

03 The rules
  • No unverified numbers. Tesla referral benefits change by country, model, and date; this site states no benefit amount unless verified against Tesla's official source the same day, and pages carry "Last checked" dates.
  • Referral honesty. The site's own referral code is capped for 2026 and the site says so everywhere it appears, instead of promising benefits it cannot deliver.
  • Disclosed affiliations. Gear pages use Amazon affiliate links, marked as such, with a disclosure at the top. Only products the owner actually bought and kept appear there. Nothing is sponsored.
  • No financial advice. Stock and merger coverage grades public evidence by source quality and makes no recommendations.
04 Contact

Corrections, questions, or something factually wrong on this site? Reach out on X at @TeslablogEU. Corrections are applied to the page itself, with the "Last checked" date updated.
